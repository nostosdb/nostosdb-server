# Interaction policy

This repository does not operate an external contribution community or
confidential conduct process. Public issue or feedback surfaces, if enabled,
are evaluation channels and not contribution forums.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, and disclosure of private
information are unacceptable. The copyright holder may moderate or close
project-controlled discussions under the hosting platform's rules. Do not send
confidential conduct reports or personal data through public issues. Security
reports use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), never a public conduct report.
